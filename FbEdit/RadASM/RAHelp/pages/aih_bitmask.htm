BitMask by Donkey

Originally a stand alone app by Delight, re-written by Donkey as an AddIn

This AddIn appears as a button on the toolbar. It is a bitmask generator that allows you to generate a mask from numbers in your code or numbers you enter and insert it in your project. It also doubles as a handy hex to decimal converter.

Add the Following line to RadASM.INI

In the [AddIns] section

XX=Bitmask.dll,1

Where XX is the next available AddIn number.

Copy Bitmask.dll to the AddIns folder
