Features:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
o Frequency counter.
  - Counts frequency up to 12MHz.
  - 1Hz resolution.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Port pins used:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PA.01				TIM2 Input for frequency counter.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Port pins used by discovery board:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PA.00				User button input.
PA.13				TMS/SWDIO.
PA.14				TCK/SWCLK.
PB.02				BOOT1.
PC.08				LED4 Output (Blue).
PC.09				LED3 Output (Green).
PC.14				OSC In.
PC.15				OSC Out.
PD.00				OSC In.
PD.01				OSC Out.
